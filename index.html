With over 4 years of professional experience, I've honed my skills in creating designs that not only look beautiful but also do what the clients need them to do. My journey began in 2020 with graphic design, where I developed a strong foundation in the basics of design.

As time passed, I expanded my expertise to prints and explored web design, allowing me to create cohesive digital and physical experiences from concept to execution. I believe that great design is one that resonates with the target audience well not just to the designer.

Logo Design (Ajisefashion)

Ajisefashion's logo is a monogram conceptualized from the letters A and F in the brand name. The logo was carefully crafted by combing the initials with a slant stroke and a box around them. All of these elements combine in a unique way to make the logo (which is a representation of the brand itself) appear corporate, modern and stylish.

Logo Ajisefashion Visual Identity Fashion Brand

Poster Design (NAPS WEEK '24)

The National Association of Physics Students, University of Ibadan Chapter were going to celeberate their departmental week. I had to come up with a creative poster that would be the major visual content for publicity ahead of the grand week. The assignment was clear, it has to be a beautiful piece that embodied Physics at the core of its concept (I chose astrophysics concept because it aligns more with the theme). The delivery was made and the representatives of the association were satisfied.

Departmental Week Poster Scientific Physics

Logo Design (Arktee)

Arktee Fabrics and Styles brand needed rebranding (particularly a redesign of their logo). The assignment was to create a logo that works across multiple media, is corporate and has the brand name on display (meaning logo types like abstract marks or mascots are not top options here). After communicating with the client to ensure clarity and clear direction, I delivered this creative piece. It works in coloured version, it works in black and white, it is scalable (making it functional across multiple media).
